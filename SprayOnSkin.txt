I want to develop spray-on skin protector for athletes and motorcycle riders. The idea is that if they have a fall, the protected areas will protect the skin from tearing. The spray on skin protector was inspired by the liquid bandage products that seal the skin from being exposed to bacteria than can worsen an injury. The spray on skin protector must have the protective qualities while not being damaging to the wearer's skin. The correct chemical make-up must be determined to ensure the product is safe. Another concern is how to remove the spray on skin once it is no longer needed. In this case, the spray on skin must have a removal solution that breaks down the spray on skin while not damaging the wearer's own skin. Os, that means to implement the spray on skin product, a spray on skin remover product must be co-developed.
